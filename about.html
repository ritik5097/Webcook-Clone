Abou Us

   

Webcooks is Punjab’s Leading IT Training institute established in 2019. Webcooks was established with the aim of digitally upskilling Punjab. Our sole aim is to bridge the skill gap between the educational curriculum and the industry demand. We have trained more than,1200 students through online & offline mediums. As one of Punjab's Best Training Institute, we create a fun learning environment for our students

The Webcooks DIGITAL UPSKILLING is focused on training and mentorship, where one can learn to work in a professional environment following the agile methodology, developing technical skills as well as soft skills.
